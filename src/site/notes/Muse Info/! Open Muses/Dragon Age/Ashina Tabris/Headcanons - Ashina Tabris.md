# Universal Headcanons
> [!headcanon]- Adaia Tabris née Arainai - Antivan Crow
> - *Ashina's mother*
> 
> Prior to her fleeing to Ferelden, Adaia was a Crow of House Arainai, one of many of the rank and file assassins within the House. When she became pregnant in 9:06 Dragon, she escapes, fleeing to Denerim, where she meets Cyrion and agrees to marry him. She never tells him or anyone else of her true past, or how she learned her skills as a rogue. On the 30th of Harvestmere, she gives birth to Ashina.
> 
> In 9:24 Dragon, the Crows complete their contract for her, killing her some 18 years after her escape. Her death is witnessed by Ashina, age 17 at the time.
