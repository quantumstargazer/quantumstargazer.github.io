# Universal *Headcanons*
## Ancient Memories
> [!headcanon]+ Ancient Memories ( Multiverse )
> > *Conditional:* **Veteran of the Fifth Blight**
> 
> Ashina frees the trapped spirit of an ancient elvhen Arcane Warrior in the Brecilian Forest, gaining the knowledge of its martial discipline *and* of the ***ancient elvhen language***.
> 
> This functions similarly to the effects of drinking from the Well of Sorrows, only far more limited, as it's only one spirit's memories and the spirit itself is not actually a part of it. But she does get vague impressions when interacting with elvhen ruins and artifacts, as well as ancient elves — including the Evanuris themselves — as a result of being in possession of these ancient memories herself now.

### 9:05 - 9:09 Dragon
#### Mother's Secrets
> [!headcanon]+ Mother's Secrets ( Dragon Age )
> - *Approximate Date:* **9:06 Dragon**
> - *Location:* **Denerim - Ferelden**
> 
> Adaia harbors many secrets, refusing to tell even Cyrion just exactly *where* she learned to fight, especially in the methods that she uses as a rogue. Adaia is an Antivan Crow who runs away from House Arainai in the early months of 9:06 Dragon when she realises she is pregnant, wishing for a life outside of the Crows for her child, even knowing that should the Crows track her down, they'll kill her, and if her child has been born by then, likely steal her away to Antiva if they learn of them. When she marries Cyrion Tabris, she is not so far along in her pregnancy that it can't pass as his child she bears, but Cyrion isn't even concerned, and raises Ashina as his own once she is born, and he is the only father she has ever known.
