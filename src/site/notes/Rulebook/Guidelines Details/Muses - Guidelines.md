# Specify Muse
**I strongly prefer when *you* choose which of my muses you wish to interact with.** This largely stems from experience with people frequently dropping interactions without saying a word about doing so because they're not *actually* interested in that muse and/or verse, and rather than just *saying so*, they run the risk of my eventually refusing to interact with them anymore when everything is frequently dropped instead.
